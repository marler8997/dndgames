Fumble:
- DC 10 reflex save

Harrow Points:
- you get 3 harrow points per book (they don't carry over)
- 1st book, harrow points allow you to Re-roll Dex based rolls
    reflex save, initiative, acrobatics, stealth, ride, escape artist, fly

Open Door:
- Normally a Move Action, we treat this as a Swift Action

Message Spell:
- duration is an open line of communication
- you have to send a sound through the spell for it to activate
- the way reply works, is the person receiving the message must intentionally be replying to whatever heard
- each time you cast, it replaces any existing connections
